## Chapter 4 Provision to Third Parties and Outsourcing

### Article 5 (Provision to Third Parties)
Except as required by law, the Organization will not provide personal information to third parties without the individual’s consent.

### Article 6 (Outsourcing of Handling)
The Organization may outsource all or part of the handling of personal information to external parties for the purpose of operating and maintaining the Service. In such cases, the Organization will appropriately supervise the contractors.

## Chapter 5 Security Management

### Article 7 (Security Measures)
The Organization will take necessary and appropriate measures—such as access restrictions, encryption, and record retention—to prevent leakage, loss, or damage of personal information and otherwise ensure its secure management.

## Chapter 6 Retention Period

### Article 8 (Retention Period and Deletion)
When 30 days have passed since the User’s last access date, the Organization will completely and irreversibly delete **information other than the amino acid sequences entered**. The chat screen at the time of input and the output results will be stored on the chat screen until the User stops using the Service.

## Chapter 7 Use of Cookies and Similar Technologies

### Article 9 (Cookies, etc.)
For the purposes of improving convenience, ensuring security, and understanding usage conditions of the Service, the Organization may use cookies and similar technologies. Users can refuse cookies via their browser settings; however, some features of the Service may not be available.

## Chapter 8 Cross-Border Transfers

### Article 10 (Handling Outside the Country)
Depending on the location of cloud services used by the Organization, personal information may be stored or processed on servers outside the country. In such cases, the Organization will take necessary measures to ensure appropriate protection of personal information in accordance with applicable laws.

## Chapter 9 Requests for Disclosure, etc.

Users may, in accordance with applicable law, request disclosure, correction, addition, deletion, suspension of use, etc., regarding their personal information held by the Organization.
